ABOUT US

“SLM Containers” is founded by a team of passionate professionals who believe in popularising the ‘Bunk House’ concept. Highly durable, sustainable, and affordable portable industrial containers/cabins are manufactured by an excellent in-house engineering team, coupled with our state of art infrastructure and high-quality raw materials.

Owing to impeccable customer service, and exceptional products, the company has gained many trusted clients. Customization of the products based on client requirements has been a key differentiator that has enabled SLM to stand apart from competitors.

Currently, bunk houses have received much needed, and widespread popularity due to their affordability and versatility. Procurement of a space to install the bunk house is the only prerequisite for commencing one’s business or stay!
